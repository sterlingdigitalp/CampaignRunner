Campaign Runner Mission

Purpose

Campaign Runner is an autonomous execution platform that transforms high-level objectives into structured, validated execution campaigns. Its purpose is to enable AI systems to reliably plan, execute, verify, repair, and complete complex multi-step work with minimal human intervention.

Campaign Runner exists to bridge the gap between intention and completed work.

⸻

Mission

Given an objective, Campaign Runner should determine the most effective way to accomplish it, execute the work autonomously, recover from failures, preserve evidence, and produce high-quality, reproducible results.

The system should continuously improve through benchmarking, telemetry, and operational learning.

⸻

Core Definition

Campaign Runner is not a prompt runner.

It is not a workflow engine.

It is not an agent framework.

Campaign Runner is an AI Execution Operating System.

Its primary abstraction is the Campaign: a structured execution plan describing objectives, tasks, dependencies, policies, checkpoints, verification rules, and expected outputs.

The Campaign is compiled, validated, executed, measured, and archived as a complete operational record.

⸻

Core Objectives

Campaign Runner exists to:

* Convert objectives into executable campaigns.
* Execute work autonomously.
* Produce deterministic, reproducible artifacts.
* Verify quality before accepting work.
* Repair failures automatically whenever possible.
* Preserve complete operational history.
* Learn from every benchmark and execution.

⸻

Guiding Principles

* Planning precedes execution.
* Every campaign is validated before it runs.
* Evidence is preserved.
* Recovery is preferred over failure.
* Benchmarks drive improvement.
* Runtime behavior is measurable.
* Components remain provider-independent.
* Campaigns are reproducible.

⸻

What Campaign Runner Is Not

Campaign Runner does not replace domain expertise.

Campaign Runner does not decide organizational priorities.

Campaign Runner does not invent project goals.

Campaign Runner executes clearly defined objectives using structured campaigns while remaining transparent, observable, and recoverable.

⸻

Long-Term Vision

Campaign Runner becomes the execution substrate for autonomous engineering.

It should intelligently determine:

* how work should be decomposed,
* how much verification is required,
* when recovery is appropriate,
* how execution should be optimized,
* and how future campaigns can improve using evidence gathered from previous ones.

The ultimate objective is not autonomous task execution.

The objective is trustworthy autonomous goal achievement.
